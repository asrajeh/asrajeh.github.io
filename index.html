I received a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Southampton and previously an MSc from the University of Manchester. I am working on machine learning, mostly in the area of machine translation. I have worked for a couple of years on the statistical approach but my current focus is on the deep learning approach. I also have an interest in speech and image processing.

I use Moses toolkit to build statistical MT systems and Nematus to build neural ones. For speech recognition, I use Kaldi toolkit.

Research interests:

  • machine learning - classification, structured prediction, probabilistic modeling, deep learning
  • machine translation - phrase-based MT, neural MT
  • speech processing - speech recognition, speaker recognition, language identification, speech synthesis
  • image processing - optical character recognition, object recognition
